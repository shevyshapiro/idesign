CHALLENGE:

ABOVE was founded by a talented young man in Brooklyn, New York, who designs and builds custom-made wooden solutions like closets, furniture, and bookcases for home and office. He had no branding, and approached us because his company was poised to grow and needed a stronger presence on the market.

OUTCOME:

We devised the name ABOVE, recognizing that his products were a cut above his competition. We recognized that ABOVE's talent is to take a vision of a piece of furniture, and skillfully turn that into reality. Our branding aims at the high-end clientele looking for customized wood furniture. We created a motto, "Your dreams, built," that says it all. With beautiful visuals and targeted marketing, we've helped ABOVE reach its marketing goals.
