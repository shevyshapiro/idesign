CHALLENGE:

When Yittu Fink, the founder of Nomi, a wig company based in city, state, approached us, she needed a strong brand identity to succeed in a competitive market. She wanted to convey primarily that her clients – casual, low-maintenance women looking for a stress-free buying experience could buy stunning, high-quality wigs at a great price. Our challenge was to create a brand that would project quality and affordability.

OUTCOME:

We brought that message alive with the “Beautiful Like You” tagline and a brand identity conveying simplicity and style. A sharp website and targeted marketing campaign helped to further bolster the brand. Nomi's business has since rapidly expanded throughout the New York City tri-state area.
